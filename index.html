Honoring Legacies in Westminster Since 1906

A memorial is more than a marker; it is a commemorative focal point for family and friends to visit for generations. Since 1906, our goal has been to provide a personalized tribute that serves as a lasting anchor for your loved one's final resting spot.

Mathias Monuments has been serving families with dignity and care for generations. We specialize in crafting beautiful, lasting memorials that celebrate the lives of those we've lost. Our skilled artisans combine traditional craftsmanship with modern techniques to create monuments that stand the test of time.

Custom Design

From favorite Bible passages to custom hobby icons, we design every stone in-house to your exact specifications.

Transparent Value

We work directly with families. Because our team is not commission-based, we provide the highest quality craftsmanship at the most competitive value.

Expert Guidance

We handle the technical details—from cemetery size restrictions to installation methods—so you can focus on the tribute itself.

Monument Restoration

Expert restoration services to preserve existing monuments and bring them back to their original beauty.

Professional Engraving

Meticulous engraving with attention to detail, ensuring every letter and design element is perfectly crafted.
